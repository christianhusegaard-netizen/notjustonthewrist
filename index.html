About

The man behind the wrist

I am a Dane with an abiding passion for watches in all their forms — sport watches, dress watches, chronographs, and everything in between.

Not Just On The Wrist is a purely personal endeavour. I have no commercial involvement with any other websites, retailers, or grey market dealers. I don't accept sponsorships, and I don't broker watches. I write because I find horology genuinely fun and endlessly interesting — and because a good watch, like a good story, deserves to be shared.

This site is my way of connecting with fellow enthusiasts: people who understand why a particular dial texture catches the light just so, why a well-tapered bracelet matters, or why some watches simply feel right the moment they touch your skin. Whether you're a seasoned collector or just beginning to notice the world on your wrist, I hope you'll find something here that resonates.

Every piece I write comes from personal experience and honest opinion. The watches I cover are ones I've held, worn, and spent time thinking about. No press releases, no affiliate links — just one collector's perspective, offered freely to anyone who shares the fascination.

— Not Just on the Wrist, Copenhagen
